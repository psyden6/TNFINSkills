About us

The Apex Skill Develop Centre for BFSI is a public – private initiative by the Tamil Nadu government and MSEFSL, Chennai, the lead implementation partner (LIP). We work to help Tamil Nadu attain its humungous potential. We seek to catalyze the creation of most skilled professionals in the banking and financial services domain

What we do

We work with the industry, students, job seekers, working professionals - directly and through creative partnerships with educational institutions, government and private providers  - for offering world class skilling / upskilling and reskilling courses . Our aim, thus, is creating a predictable pipeline for the BFSI sector talent needs
